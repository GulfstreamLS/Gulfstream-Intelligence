# PROJECT GX-202: Clinical Study Protocol Summary

## 1. Study Overview
Project GX-202 is a Phase 2, multi-center, randomized, double-blind study designed to evaluate the safety and efficacy of GX-202 in 50 patients with mild-to-moderate inflammatory conditions. The study duration is 12 weeks.

## 2. Patient Selection
Inclusion Criteria:
- Age 18-65 years.
- Diagnosis of mild-to-moderate inflammation.
- Ability to provide informed consent.
